Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Smart Placard ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use our Android application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Smart Placard does not collect any personal data. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. All content you create — including placards, text, and images — is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not use analytics, crash-reporting services, or any other mechanism that would send data from your device to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no information for us to use, share, or process. Your content remains entirely under your control on your own device.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data created within Smart Placard is stored locally on your device using the standard storage mechanisms provided by Android. We have no access to this data. You are responsible for the security of your device and the data stored on it.

If you uninstall the app or clear the app's data through your device settings, all locally stored content will be permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

Smart Placard is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information as part of the distribution process. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services – for app distribution. (Google Privacy Policy)

5. Children's Privacy

Smart Placard does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The Service is suitable for general audiences; however, children should use the app under appropriate parental supervision.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no personal data held by us for you to access, correct, delete, or port. All your data lives on your own device and can be managed or deleted directly from there.

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us using the details in the Contact Us section below.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Content you create is stored solely on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data will permanently remove all locally stored content.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, sending you a notification within the app. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
